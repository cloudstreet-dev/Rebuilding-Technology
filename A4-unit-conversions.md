## Temperature

### Conversion Formulas

**Fahrenheit to Celsius**:
```
°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
```

**Celsius to Fahrenheit**:
```
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
```

**Celsius to Kelvin**:
```
K = °C + 273.15
```

## Memory Aids

### Quick Mental Conversions

| To convert | Remember |
|------------|----------|
| km to miles | Divide by 1.6 (or × 0.6) |
| miles to km | Multiply by 1.6 |
| kg to pounds | Multiply by 2.2 |
| pounds to kg | Divide by 2.2 |
| liters to gallons | Divide by 4 (rough) |
| gallons to liters | Multiply by 4 (rough) |
| °C to °F | Double and add 30 (rough) |
| °F to °C | Subtract 30, halve (rough) |
| inches to cm | Multiply by 2.5 |
| cm to inches | Divide by 2.5 |

### Useful Equivalents to Memorize

- 1 inch = 25.4 mm (exact)
- 1 foot ≈ 30 cm
- 1 meter ≈ 39 inches ≈ 3.3 feet
- 1 kilometer ≈ 0.6 miles
- 1 mile ≈ 1.6 km
- 1 pound ≈ 0.45 kg
- 1 kilogram ≈ 2.2 pounds
- 1 gallon (US) ≈ 3.8 liters
- 1 liter ≈ 1 quart (roughly)
- 1 horsepower ≈ 746 watts ≈ 3/4 kW
- Water freezes at 32°F = 0°C
- Water boils at 212°F = 100°C
- 1 atmosphere ≈ 14.7 psi ≈ 1 bar

---

**Tip**: When converting between systems, always double-check critical measurements. Use exact conversions for precision work, mental shortcuts for estimation.
